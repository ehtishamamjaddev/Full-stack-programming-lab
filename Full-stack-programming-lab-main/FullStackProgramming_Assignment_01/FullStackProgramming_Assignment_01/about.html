Our Story

Built in Islamabad,
Designed for Pakistan

HOTSPRING was founded in Islamabad with a clear purpose: to bring world-class wellness solutions to Pakistani homes and businesses. What started as a vision shared by a small team of engineers has grown into one of the country's most trusted names in premium hot tubs and spas.

Our team combines precision engineering with thoughtful design, sourcing high-grade materials and rigorously testing every unit before delivery. We believe relaxation should never be a compromise — it should be engineered.

Today, HOTSPRING serves over 2,500 customers across Pakistan, from private residences in Islamabad and Lahore to luxury resorts in the Northern Areas. We continue to raise the standard of what a home spa experience can be.
